President Goku is the main character of Dragon Ball America. He becomes president after President Lane sees him defeat Piccolo. Goku stays president for 26 years, until Goten takes his place (Gohan is too busy taking care of Pan). He is the best president on the show, and making jokes about power a lot gives him popularity.
